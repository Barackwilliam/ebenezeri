Welcome Eben-ezeri Mind Education

Eben-ezeri Mind Education (EME) is a leading educational consultancy dedicated to enhancing mental health awareness and personal development in Tanzanian communities.
Officially registered under BL01396912024-2500027304
EME is headquartered in Bunju "B" Street, Mabwepande Ward, Kinondoni District, Dar es Salaam.
Our mission is to equip individuals with the knowledge and tools needed to overcome psychological challenges, build emotional resilience, and foster overall well-being.

EME primarily supports marginalized communities, youth aged 8 to 30 years, and individuals struggling with mental health issues. Through innovative and accessible educational programs, we strive to create a society where mental wellness and personal growth are prioritized.

Join with us

Main Objective

To improve the mental well-being and economic resilience of individuals and communities through accessible education, training, and support programs that foster psychological strength, personal growth, and financial independence.

Our Goals

To establish a mentally resilient and economically self-sufficient society by promoting mind education, mental health awareness, and entrepreneurship training, ensuring sustainable and long-term development for Tanzanian communities.

Vision

To create a transformative impact in communities through mind education. We aim to empower individuals by enhancing their psychological and cognitive abilities, enabling them to make informed decisions and lead fulfilling lives.

Mission

To empower individuals and communities by providing essential knowledge and tools that promote mental well-being, personal growth, and cognitive development, ultimately fostering a healthier and more prosperous society.
